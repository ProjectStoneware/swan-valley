Two counters, one roof.

Most mornings start at the espresso bar and end somewhere down the aisles. Swan Valley is both halves of a small-town stop — caffeine on one side, the thrill of the hunt on the other.

The Brew

Pulled, poured, worth the drive

Espresso drinks, drip by the cup, and a rotating board of seasonal sips. Small batch, fair prices, and a barista who'll likely remember how you take it by the second visit.

  • Espresso
  • Lattes
  • Drip coffee
  • Seasonal specials
  • Pastries
The Market

Aisles worth wandering

A flea market that turns over often — antiques, oddities, handmade goods, and the occasional thing you didn't know you needed. Stock changes, so the find you skip today may be gone tomorrow.

  • Antiques
  • Vintage
  • Handmade
  • Collectibles
  • Local vendors

Most everything lands between $1 and $10 — easy to grab a coffee and a treasure on the same stop.
